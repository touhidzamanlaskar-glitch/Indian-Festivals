About Indian Festivals

India is known as the Land of Festivals. Every festival reflects the country's rich history, culture, traditions, and spiritual values. From the colorful celebrations of Holi to the dazzling lights of Diwali, every festival brings people together in the spirit of joy, harmony, and unity.

Festivals are celebrated across different religions and regions, making India one of the most culturally diverse nations in the world.
